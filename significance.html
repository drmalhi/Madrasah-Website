MADRASAH INSTITUTION:

Project Title:

UNDERWRITE MADRASAH: The Immigrant Children and Poverty


Conflict Experienced in Madrasah:

Based on our research, the teaching techniques of narrow teachers and the lack of financial essentials to support the primary and secondary level of education were the issues and concerns experienced in the Madrasah Institution.

Our Project Aims to:

1.) Support the educational system of the community through better teaching teahcniques.

2.) Educate the youth on the proper basics of behaviour as such standard manners (way of speaking, way of communicating, way of doing basic gestures,
all in a respectful way.

3.) Spread out the importance of having proper behavior.

How can the project be beneficial to the institution?

Our project is very important as it serves to provide the institution youth the importance of proper communication skills. Lets take for example, you need to discuss about a serious issue in your community, you need your voice to be heard out however if you wont be taught the proper gestures in approaching higher authorities, most will believe that something your fighting for is truly a waste. This is a reason why our proposal, UNDERWRITE MADRASAH: The Immigrant Children and Poverty seeks to serve these youth the education which will be benefitial for the bettterment of their future.

How can it affect the atenean's ?

This project affects us the atenean's in a positive side as well as we learn the core values which the school mission's to. Not just
we teach the youth of Madrasah communication skills, we as well learn how to communicate with patience, and
observe peacefulness in a class discussion.

How is it helpfull to the public in general?

Our project can affect the public in general as it influences the public to always be a helpful hand towards the ones in need. Madrasah Institution is a huge resort to those in need, however through the conflicts they have been experiencing it was now our role as atenean's to be thoughtful.
